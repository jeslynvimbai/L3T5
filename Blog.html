Aquarius

Aquarius is the eleventh astrological sign of the zodiac, originating from the constellation Aquarius. It is an air sign represented by the water bearer. Aquarians are known for their independent and innovative nature.

Leo

Leo is the fifth astrological sign of the zodiac, originating from the constellation Leo. It is a fire sign represented by the lion. Leos are known for their charismatic and confident personality.
